About me

Background & expertise

My recent professional experience includes developing internal data and AI systems for order ingestion, database access, agentic assistance, and production process analysis. I combine automation with validation, privacy controls, human review, and maintainable software interfaces.

I hold an MSc in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence from Eindhoven University of Technology and a BSc in Data Science from TU/e and Tilburg University. My academic work includes safe reinforcement learning, NLP classification, time-series modelling, and graph-data systems.

Based in Eindhoven, I enjoy turning complex technical problems into reproducible workflows, measurable insights, and practical tools for both technical and non-technical users.
