About me

Background & expertise

I'm a Master's student in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence at Eindhoven University of Technology, with a Bachelor's degree in Data Science from TU/e and Tilburg University. Currently pursuing my thesis on "Reward-Free Safe Reinforcement Learning Exploration using Different Entropy Measures."

With hands-on experience in machine learning, data engineering, and AI systems, I specialize in building end-to-end solutions for real-world problems. My work spans fraud detection pipelines, NLP applications, time-series analysis, and LLM-based systems, with a focus on production-ready implementations using modern MLOps practices.

Based in Eindhoven, The Netherlands, I combine academic rigor with practical engineering to deliver scalable, reliable AI solutions that drive business impact.
